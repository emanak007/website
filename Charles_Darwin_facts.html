Charles Darwin is a very famous biologist who developed the theory evolution. He was born on the 12th of Febuary 1809.
In 1831 Darwin set sail on the HMS Beagle, a naval survey ship. Darwin was there to collect plant and animal specimens from the countries and islands the ship visited.
The voyage took five years. For Charles Darwin, the most important part of the journey was the time spent in the Galapagos Islands.
